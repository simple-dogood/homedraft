About YSMD


Are you ready to take the thousands of ideas that you have been saving from HGTV, Pintrest and more and apply it to a room in your home? Most people find themselves delaying on the execution of their home plan as they find the process to be overwhelming for any of the following reasons:

  • How and where to begin
  • The vast number of home décor websites and stores
  • Uncertainty of the cost associated with turning your idea into a reality
  • Finding the time to put your ideas together or shop due to your busy schedule
The good news is that this is normal and you have already taken the first step by looking for a designer to assist you with the process. Regardless of the size of the project I am here to guide you through the steps that will allow you to take the most effective approach in creating a cohesive and functional space that will fit your budget. I grew up with a father who was a housing inspector and general contractor and a mother who was a project engineer. My first taste of designing first introduced to me by them. When I was young, I assisted my parents with their home renovation plans and budget restraints for their projects. I worked for free (like most kids who worked for their parents) but to them, as they love to remind me, it was not free labor but a learning experience. Well…..my parents were right. After raising my two beautiful daughters with my husband and working several years in the corporate world I challenged myself to return to school and get certified in my one true passion…..interior design and home staging. And so, I thank my parents for the lessons and I thanks my daughters and husband for the loving support.
